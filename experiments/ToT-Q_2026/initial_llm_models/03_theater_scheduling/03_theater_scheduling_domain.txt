In the context of a theater organization, various plays are performed in one or more theaters, each identified by its own location, for example "The Northwest Theater". A play may have multiple scheduled sessions, and each session occurs on a scheduled date. Plays are identified by a unique name and is either a musical or an opera. Furthermore, each play may take place during the day or at night.

Each play is written by one or more authors, who are uniquely identified by name. Plays involve participants each also identified by name, and the same participant may act in multiple plays. For each play session, the starting time and an ending time is recorded, and participants can act in one or more sessions. Both authors and participant can be the same person, who may write a play and participate in its performance.
