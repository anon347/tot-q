In an academic institution, students enroll in various courses offered by the university. Each student is uniquely identified by a student code. An enrollment in the course requires the specific date on which it occurred for administrative purposes.

Courses are supported by Teaching Assistants (TAs), who are identified by their first and last names. A TA may assist in multiple different courses, and the specific role of a TA within a course is defined by an assignment, which describes the details and responsibilities assigned to them. Regarding courses logistics, they consist of one or more lab sections. Each section occurs at a specific location and time slot with start and end times.
