In an e-commerce retail organization, customers are registered with unique ids to place orders that includes multiple products. An order also has a unique identifier and consists of one or more products, which are described by their name and description. When the customer completes the order, it is necessary to specify the quantity requested and the price for the item is updated.

The retailer also ask customers to provide feedback by writing reviews for specific products. These reviews capture the opinion of a customer regarding a product, including the date it was written and a rating score. Finally, the marketing department organizes promotional campaigns. A product may be included in various promotions over time. Whenever a product is part of a campaign, specific terms are established for that offer, such as the applicable discount rate and a validity period defined by a start and end date.
